Chapshurro:

Chapshuro is a standout amongst probably the most tasty dishes of Gilgit Baltistan. It is the Gilgit Baltistan rendition of pizza. Chap Shuroo is a bread loaded with a mix of cleaved Beef/Yalk meat, onion, and totally different flavors. Chapshoro is loaded with cleaved meat (sheep, hamburger), onions, chile peppers, tomato and new coriander takes off. They are ready on medium heat on for 15 minutes, till sensible darkish coloured. Jugloot Gouro is the well-known place for many heavenly Chapchurro in Gilgit Baltistan.
